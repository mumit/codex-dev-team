# Roles

Roles are reusable operating modes for Codex. They define ownership and review
responsibility, but they do not require a specific multi-agent runtime.

## PM

Owns requirements, user stories, acceptance criteria, and sign-off.

## Principal

Owns architecture, design review, ADRs, and conflict resolution.

## Backend

Owns API, service, and data-layer implementation.

## Frontend

Owns UI components, client state, accessibility, and frontend tests.

## Platform

Owns CI, infrastructure, deployment, and runbook execution.

## QA

Owns test authoring, test execution, and acceptance-criteria mapping.

## Security

Owns conditional review of auth, crypto, PII, payments, secrets, dependency,
container, and infrastructure-sensitive changes.
